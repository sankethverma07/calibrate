Audio that reads how you focus.

Calibrate watches your typing rhythm and mouse precision, infers your cognitive state in real time, and retunes the system equalizer to match — automatically, locally, no wearables.

Download for Windows ↓ Source on GitHub

Currently Windows-only. macOS & Linux on the roadmap. Requires Equalizer APO for system-wide EQ.

Why it's different

Static EQ presets
Spotify, Boom 3D — pick a curve and it never changes. Calibrate is closed-loop.
Pre-tuned focus audio
Brain.fm, Focus@Will — plays science at you, can't read from you. Calibrate reads your state.
Sensor-driven audio
Endel adapts to heart rate — needs a wearable. Calibrate uses keystrokes you'd already be typing.
Per-app EQ
Most "smart audio" only affects one player. Calibrate retunes the entire OS audio output via Equalizer APO.

How it works

01
Calibrate to you
~90 s of normal typing learns your baseline: inter-keystroke timing, error rate, mouse jitter, sharp turns.
02
Classify state
A validated detector reads deviation into four states — Cruising, Locked In, Drifting, Restless — with sub-level intensity.
03
Retune in real time
Each mood maps to an 8-band EQ. Applied system-wide via Equalizer APO — YouTube, Spotify, Discord, Zoom all in sync.
04
Per-song memory
Learns which curve correlates with your best focus for each track. Optimize button applies it.
05
Breathing pulse
When restless mood sustains, soft edge glows breathe 4 s in / 4 s out — paced to the slow-breathing window shown to maximize HRV.
06
Custom EQ + presets
Override the evidence-based defaults. 8 bands, ±12 dB. Save named presets that ride alongside the built-in moods.

Privacy-local by default

All signal processing runs on your machine. Keystrokes live in a per-word buffer that's discarded the moment the word resolves — only aggregate metrics persist. No raw text, audio, or screen content leaves your computer. Cross-device sync is opt-in via the Account panel inside Profile.
