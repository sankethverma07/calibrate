# CALIBRATE — Smart Equalizer
## 28-second cinematic product film · Apple Vision Pro reveal aesthetic

> Premium product film. 28 seconds. No audio. Cinematography in the lineage
> of Apple's Vision Pro reveal, AirPods Pro 2 launch, and Spike Jonze's
> HomePod "Welcome Home." Restraint above all — no music-video maximalism,
> no flashy moves. Motion-controlled smoothness, deliberate holds, soft
> motivated light, one accent per beat.

## THE WORLD

The film takes place inside an atmospheric ambient environment — never a
black void. The setting feels like the inside of a vast, climate-controlled
cinematic stage with depth and air. A soft horizon line glows at the lower
third of frame. Volumetric haze fills the space, tinted by the current mood
color, catching the camera's light like dust through a sun-shaft window. The
corners fade to slightly deeper tones than the center — natural cinematographic
vignette. The atmosphere is deeply desaturated and low-luminance (~10-15% of
full brightness) so it never competes with the hero, but it always reads as
a *room*, not a hole.

In front of this environment is an **ocean of light** — a wide horizontal
plane of ten thousand microscopic glowing dust particles. Each is a tiny
photonic point of light, self-luminous, the brightness of a distant star.
Not fireflies, not water — pure pinpoint photons, like dust caught in a
sunbeam but glowing from within. The plane undulates slowly on a smooth
multi-directional sine wave displacement, like a calm sea.

Each particle renders with **real cinema-lens character**. In focus, it
appears as a razor-sharp cool-white pinpoint (5500-7000 Kelvin) with a tiny
four-point diffraction spike — an iris artifact. Out of focus, the same
particle becomes a hexagonal bokeh disc with a bright soap-bubble rim and
subtle red/cyan chromatic fringing — a six-blade aperture iris (Cooke S4/i
lens). The hexagonal bokeh shape is essential. Most particles in any given
frame are bokeh discs; only the focal plane holds sharp pinpoints.

**The system has four moods, each with its own atmosphere:**

- **Cruising** (teal `#2dd4bf`) — a calm cool dawn-room. Deep teal
  cyclorama, faint horizon glow, subtle vertical light shafts cutting from
  above. Air feels still.
- **Locked In** (blue `#60a5fa`) — looking up from beneath a deep ocean
  surface. Cool indigo gradient with overhead caustics. Volumetric haze
  gives the air weight. Distant bioluminescent motes flicker far away.
- **Drifting** (magenta `#c084fc`) — soft Northern-Lights aurora drapes the
  upper third of frame, magenta and violet, slow-moving. Dreamy, ethereal.
- **Restless** (orange `#fb923c`) — warm sunset embers at the horizon,
  smoke haze drifting, oranges and red-browns at corners. Air charged,
  slightly hot.

Atmosphere crossfades between moods on slow 2-3 second lerps. Like a stage's
lighting designer changing the color wash between scenes.

The product is **Calibrate** — a smart equalizer that reads your focus state
and re-tunes audio. A frosted dark-glass control card materializes later in
the film, floating in this space. Eight vertical EQ sliders for frequency
bands at 60 / 170 / 310 / 600 / 1K / 3K / 6K / 12K Hz. Translucent dark
glass — absolutely **no chrome, no mirror reflections, no clearcoat shine**.
Pure frosted glass that the wave and atmosphere read through.

## THE FILM — 10 shots, 28 seconds

**0:00 – 0:03 · Open in the ocean.** Camera locked-off, 50 cm above the
wave, tilted down 12°. 50mm cinema lens, f/2. The Cruising-teal atmosphere
wraps the frame; the horizon line glows softly at the bottom third. Wave
fills lower two-thirds, centered. Focal plane sits DEEP in the field at 8m.
Far particles are razor-sharp pinpoints; foreground a creamy wall of
hexagonal bokeh with bright soap-bubble rims. Particles drift gently. The
room breathes.

**0:03 – 0:07 · Focus pulls forward.** Camera holds — no pan, no dolly. The
focal plane racks smoothly from 8m → 4m across four seconds. The viewer's
eye follows the focal plane sweeping THROUGH the wave: background dust
defocuses into creamy hexagons, midground particles crystallize into
pinpricks. The teal cyclorama begins crossfading into Locked In blue — the
room's color shifts like a sunrise. Wave amplitude swells gently.

**0:07 – 0:10 · The macro moment.** Camera lifts 10 cm for parallax depth.
Focus continues to 2m. Near foreground particles become pixel-precise stars
with diffraction spikes; everything beyond is creamy hexagonal bokeh filling
60% of frame. Locked In blue atmosphere settled — distant caustics flicker
in the upper haze. This is the macro-photography money frame, like a
pink-flower shot where one bloom is razor-sharp and the rest is dream-soft.

**0:10 – 0:12.5 · Sliders emerge.** Camera dollies back and lifts to 1.2m.
From the wave surface, eight orange pill-shaped slider handles rise into
view one by one, staggered 0.15 seconds apart. Beneath each, a thin dark
recessed track capsule materializes. Tiny frequency labels (60Hz, 170Hz,
310Hz, 600Hz, 1KHz, 3KHz, 6KHz, 12KHz) fade in below each track in muted
gray small caps. The wave continues breathing in soft bokeh below.

**0:12.5 – 0:15.5 · One slider drives the ocean.** Smash cut to an extreme
macro close-up of the 1 kHz slider handle. 85mm lens. The handle fills the
left half of frame, razor sharp. Wave dust behind dissolves to 15% opacity
— quiet. **In slow motion**, the orange handle rises from center to +3 dB
position over 2.5 seconds. As it rises, the wave's amplitude beneath that
frequency band visibly BLOOMS — particles in the corresponding region surge
upward, creating a glowing crest exactly under the slider. The atmosphere
crossfades to Drifting magenta — Northern Lights wash the upper frame in
pink — as if the gesture has changed not just the audio but the user's
focus state. A soft backlight haloes the metal slider.

**0:15.5 – 0:17.5 · All bands aligned.** Camera pulls back to a medium shot
showing all eight orange slider handles in a row with their tracks and
frequency labels. Wave breathes softly behind. Atmosphere lerps back toward
Locked In blue — the system resolves.

**0:17.5 – 0:20.5 · The card forms.** Camera continues dollying back. A
frosted dark-glass control card materializes around the eight sliders.
Translucent body at 72% opacity, heavy frost — no chrome, no mirror, no
clearcoat. The card carries "CUSTOM EQ" in orange `#fb923c` at top-left;
"Locked In preset" in white weight-300 sans-serif; four mood pill tabs at
top-right (Cruising / Locked In / Drifting / Restless, Locked In highlighted);
a tracked-small-caps subtitle; and two pill buttons at bottom-right
(Save & apply, Reset). The card fades in via opacity ramp over 2.5 seconds.
The wave and the blue atmosphere both read through the frosted glass at
low opacity.

**0:20.5 – 0:22.5 · Subtle orbit.** Slow lateral camera orbit, 30°. The
frosted-glass refraction becomes visible from the new angle — the card's
translucent depth catches light. Particles behind it cast a soft blue halo
through the glass.

**0:22.5 – 0:24.5 · Held hero.** Camera locks back to front view, slightly
higher and farther. Glass card composes at the rule-of-thirds upper-third.
Wave breathes slowly in the lower half. A single bright specular highlight
slowly travels left to right across the card's top edge over the two
seconds. Apple-discipline held composition.

**0:24.5 – 0:28 · The title.** The card and wave fade to a deep, soft
Locked In blue field (NOT pure black — the atmospheric backdrop holds,
slightly darker now). The text **"Smart Equalizer."** materializes letter
by letter in 3D space. Each letter starts edge-on (rotated 90° on Y-axis),
pushed back in Z, tilted slightly, scaled small, fully transparent. Then it
rotates and falls into final upright position with a soft scale-overshoot
bounce — cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.32, 0.55, 1) ease. Letters cascade with 0.09s
stagger and ±15ms irregularity. They land in light off-white `#f5f5f4`
with a soft cool-blue glow halation behind. After ~2.5s when all 16 letters
have settled, the subtitle **"AUDIO THAT READS HOW YOU FOCUS"** fades in
below in tracked small caps. The composition holds 0.5 seconds.

## STYLE CONSTANTS

Cinema-lens aesthetic throughout: hexagonal bokeh, six-blade aperture, ACES
filmic color grade, deep blacks at 5 IRE (never crushed), 6% film grain,
40% corner vignette. Motion-control camera smoothness — never hand-held.
Apple-level restraint in every frame. NO lens flare streaks, NO chrome
reflections on glass, NO oversaturated colors (mood saturation max 50%),
NO music-video cuts. Cuts only on motion settles or luma matches. Premium
product film aesthetic, in the Apple Vision Pro / HomePod / AirPods Pro
lineage. The room always has *air*. The product always has *weight*.
